Giacomo Zara

I am a Ph.D. Student in Computer Vision in the MHUG group at the University of Trento (Italy), where I am advised by Prof. Elisa Ricci. During summer 2023, I have been a visiting student at the University of Bristol (UK), where I was part of the MaVi group, advised by Prof. Dima Damen. I am currently a visiting researcher at IBM Ireland as part of the ELIAS project.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning, with particular respect to Video Understanding, Vision&Language, Domain Adaptation and Generalization.
