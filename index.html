Giacomo Zara

I am a Ph.D. Student in Computer Vision at the University of Trento (Italy). I am part of the MHUG group, where I am advised by Prof. Elisa Ricci.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning, with particular respect to Video Understanding, Vision&Language, Domain Adaptation and Generalization.
